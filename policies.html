Misolytic Party Policies

Our policies

Anti-cronyism and anti-elitism

The Misolytic party stands for anti-cronyism and anti-elitism. This means that we want to ensure that everything is fair and the people who are the best fit get the job.

Dispute legislation

To ensure disputes don't get out of hand, we advocate for civil legislation about disputes.

Cultural affairs

We aim to create cultural events and generally create a nice culture around the subreddit.

Diplomacy

We want to build relationships with other democracy simulators.

New users

We want to make this community as welcoming as possible for new users.
